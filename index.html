Max's Website

Hailing from Manhattan, Max grew up with a vigorous passion for expanding his own mind, and constantly tests the boundaries society puts in place. He strives to trailblaze with authenticity and with his guiding light being self expression. The Great Max offers services of all kinds. Below you will be able to see the vast prowess he posses over several important creative avenues, and how he may be of service to those seeking his enchanted work.

Musician (Pianist and Producer)

Max is currently attending Berklee College of Music, pursuing degrees in Jazz Performance and Electronic Production and Design. He has amassed a great many accolades as a pianist at the age of 20, including a semi-finalist in the Bucharest Europafest International Jazz Competition, as well as scholarship awards from Berklee. He frequently gigs in Boston, NYC, and San Fransisco. As a music producer he frequently experiments with both analog and digital music making, bridging together genres like: Jazz, RnB, Hip-Hop, Electronic, Pop, and Folk.

Aspiring Chef

In the past year or so, Max has cultivated an almost obsessive fondness to cooking. In some ways too obsessed, the dude can't stop. He'll be up at 3AM making an elaborate meal. Anyway, he's pretty good and learning quickly, some of his specialties include: Braised Short Rib Ragu Pasta, Jerk Chicken Stew, and Pork Belly Stir Fry.

Fashion Icon

Maybe not quite reaching icon status, nonetheless Max pushes boundaries with his clothes. He strives to find unique pieces and make things work together. Some words to describe his preference in style are Elegant, Eclectic, and Preppy.
